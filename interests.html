Interests.
My Favourite Song,
Love Wins All by IU.

“Love Wins All” by IU is one of my favourite songs because it feels deeply emotional and comforting at the same time. The lyrics, melody, and cinematic atmosphere make the song feel very sincere and meaningful. I love how soft yet powerful the emotions are throughout the song, especially the feeling of loving someone even through sadness, fear, and uncertainty. It is the kind of song that feels warm, nostalgic, and quietly beautiful every time I listen to it.

Golden Hour Hunter
Sunset Lover.

Capturing sunsets and sunrises became one of my favourite little hobbies because every sky feels different and special. I love the soft golden light, warm colours, and peaceful atmosphere during golden hour. Taking these photos allows me to appreciate quiet moments, nature, and beautiful memories in a simple way.

My Fav Motorsport
Formula One.

Formula One became one of my favourite sports because of the adrenaline, team strategy, speed, and emotions behind every race. I enjoy watching the intense competition between drivers and teams, especially during dramatic race moments and qualifying sessions. My favourite drivers are Max Verstappen, Kimi Antonelli, and Lando Norris because they all bring different energy and personalities to the sport, which makes watching Formula One even more exciting and enjoyable for me. Whether it's chaotic team radios, unexpected podium moments, or late-night race weekends, Formula One somehow always keeps me emotionally invested and entertained every single time.
